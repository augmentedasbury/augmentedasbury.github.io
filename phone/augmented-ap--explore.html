Augmented Asbury Park is a free mobile experience involving the reconstructions of key historic landmarks in augmented reality on the Asbury Park boardwalk in New Jersey and off-site. Scroll below to try it out!

STEP 1: Download the free Augment app on your phone or tablet.

STEP 2: Open the Augment app on your phone or tablet, and scan one of the images below.

You will need two screens for this experience, one to display the images, the other to see the 3D content. So, if you are looking at this website from your phone, pull out your tablet, laptop, or a friend's phone and bring this site up as well.
